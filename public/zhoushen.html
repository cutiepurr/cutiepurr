Zhou Shen is a Chinese singer known for super high male voice. His voice is often descirbed as somewhat between a female's voice and a little boy's. Because of this, he has lots of struggles to make the public accept his unique voice, especially when he just debuted:((.

What do I love about him?

His voice

Definitely his voice is the top reason why I love Zhou Shen. It's ethereal, pure like an angel. I feel like I'm in heaven whenever I hear his songs. His voice always helps me stay calm and peaceful.

What's the most impressive thing is Zhou Shen can do a lot of singing styles and genres. He mainly sings pop music, sometimes also combines opera, folk, Chinese opera (xiqu) elements (Fun fact: Zhou Shen studied Bel Canto (often used in opera) in college). He often sings romantic songs with Chinese traditional element, but also sings 'stronger' songs with rock and roll element, or maybe basically more modern pop songs.

Zhou Shen is capable of imitating different voices such as crying toddler, loli, Siri, switchboard, dog, screaming pig, etc:))

His hilarity

Hmmm, I don't know where to start. He's joking with fans in his concerts. He's joking when he's interviewed. He's joking when doing a TV show. He's joking by altering the lyrics. His joke also becomes memes among Chinese netizens. The most popular one might be 'Just leave me alone'.

Watching TV shows that he did is currently one of my entertainment activities!

His sincerity

He's funny, and at the same time, he's sincere. I often hear people say that Zhou Shen is like 'a pure flow of water' in the Chinese showbiz (haha celebrities have lots of dramas). And I think it's true, because at least from what I see how he interacts with people, with other singers, with his fans, with the production teams, he's a sincere and respectful person.

One of his iconic traits is after singing, he'll say 'Thank you'. It may sound pretty normal, but if you watch a large number of him singing on TV shows and stuff, it'll be strange enough. Sometimes I want to burst into laugh when he does that after singing a super sad song, because it just doesn't fit the mood!

I know that because he's currently my idol, he looks good in every aspect, so I hope that he can still be that 'pure flow of water'.
