Just kidding. Zhou Shen is a Chinese singer known for his countertenor voice aka super high male voice. Different from other countertenor singers, his voice sounds more like a young 10y/o boy or sometimes a female. Because of this, he has lots of struggles to make the public accept his unique voice until now though he makes it to be one of the most famous celebrities in China (Forbes Top 50 Chinese Celebrities if my memory serves it right!)

His best known song is Big Fish (YouTube). A beautiful and soft song.

He joined lots of TV shows and sang lots of OSTs. Most of my favorite Zhou Shen's songs are from TV shows.

How did I know about Zhou Shen?

Through this one (more precisely, the first 30 secs:))):

So I passed by this video while scrolling Facebook videos (it's addictive, you know). It was a ten-minute video, and since I used to be not that interested in Chinese songs, I only listened to the first 30 seconds.

My brain, however, kept playing the song for some hours after I scrolled by. It was just stuck in my head! Luckily, I've already hit the like button, so it was not that hard to find it.

This video is a part of a music show where singers wore a mask and the judges have to guess who the singer is. So, felt quite surprised after I re-found the video and knew it was a male singer called Zhou Shen who sang it. That's when I started being a fan of him!
